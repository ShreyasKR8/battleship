Instructions

  • Objective: Sink all of your opponent's ships before they sink yours. Players take turns guessing the location of the opponent's ships by selecting cells to "attack."
  • Placing Ships: At the start, each player places their ships on their own grid, either manually or randomly. Ships must be placed horizontally or vertically and cannot overlap or extend beyond the grid.
  • Gameplay: On your turn:
    • Choose a cell on your opponent's board to attack.
    • If you hit a ship, it is marked, and you can attempt to sink it by hitting all its parts.
    • The game ends when all parts of one player's ships are destroyed.
